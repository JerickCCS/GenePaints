About Me

I'm Gene, and I've been immersed in the world of arts for a solid 10 years now. Over this time, I've had the privilege of exploring various forms of artistic expression and honing my skills in different mediums.

Projects

Celestial Elevation"Celestial Elevation" transports the viewer to a mesmerizing world where the ethereal beauty of the Milky Way is unveiled above a majestic mountain landscape. The night sky is a canvas of celestial wonders, with a river of stars flowing across the vast expanse. The mountains, bathed in the soft glow of moonlight, stand as ancient sentinels beneath the cosmic ballet.

Ethereal Peaks at Dusk A mesmerizing painting capturing the sublime beauty of towering mountains bathed in the warm hues of a setting sun. The sky transitions from a vibrant palette of oranges and pinks to tranquil blues, casting a gentle glow on the majestic peaks. The play of light and shadows creates a serene atmosphere, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in the tranquil harmony of nature's twilight embrace.

Skills

Impressionism, Abstract Expressionism, Cubism, Surrealism and Pop Art

Education

Central Philippines State University - College of Fine Arts

Experience

My journey began with a fascination for traditional painting. I spent countless hours experimenting with colors, textures, and techniques, gradually finding my own unique style. As the years passed, my interests expanded beyond canvas, leading me to delve into digital art and graphic design. Embracing technology opened up a whole new realm of possibilities, allowing me to blend traditional and modern approaches seamlessly.
